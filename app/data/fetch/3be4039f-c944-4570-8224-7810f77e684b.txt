<p>The <b>2016–17 Tulane Green Wave men's basketball team</b> represented <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/Tulane_University">Tulane University</a> during the <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2016–17_NCAA_Division_I_men's_basketball_season">2016–17 NCAA Division I men's basketball season</a>. The Green Wave, led by first-year head coach <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/Mike_Dunleavy_Sr.">Mike Dunleavy Sr.</a>, played their home games at <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/Devlin_Fieldhouse">Devlin Fieldhouse</a> in <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/New_Orleans">New Orleans, Louisiana</a> as third-year members of the <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/American_Athletic_Conference">American Athletic Conference</a>. They finished the season 6–25, 3–15 in AAC play to finish in tenth place. They lost in the first round of the <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2017_American_Athletic_Conference_Men's_Basketball_Tournament">AAC Tournament</a> to <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2016–17_Tulsa_Golden_Hurricane_men's_basketball_team">Tulsa</a>.</p><h2 id="Previous_season">Previous season</h2><p>The <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2015–16_Tulane_Green_Wave_men's_basketball_team">Green Wave</a> finished the season 12–22, 3–15 in American Athletic play to finish in last place. They defeated <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2015–16_UCF_Knights_men's_basketball_team">UCF</a> and <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2015–16_Houston_Cougars_men's_basketball_team">Houston</a> in the <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2016_American_Athletic_Conference_Men's_Basketball_Tournament">American Athletic Tournament</a> to advance to the semifinals where they lost to <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/2015–16_Memphis_Tigers_men's_basketball_team">Memphis</a>.</p><p>Following the season, Tulane fired head coach <a href="http://dbpedia.org/resource/Ed_Conroy_(basketball)">Ed Conroy</a>. On March 25, 2016, the school hired Mike Dunleavy Sr. as head coach.</p><h2 id="Departures">Departures</h2><h3 id="Incoming_Transfers">Incoming Transfers</h3><h2 id="Incoming_recruits">Incoming recruits</h2><h2 id="Roster">Roster</h2><h2 id="Schedule_and_results">Schedule and results</h2>
